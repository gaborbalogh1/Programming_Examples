
To Plan your Route 
Add a New Customer or 
Select and Exisiting Customer 
Click the Add to Map Search Button to 
Build your List.

Once you have added all the Customers 
to the list Select Travel Distance and Driving 
From The Drop Down Lists 
Select Calculate to see the Round Trip Time 
and The Distance Covered in the Text Box.

To Do Round Trip Ensure you add your Home
Post Code to the end of the list when building your route.
